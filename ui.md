# Testimonial Slider

This section showcases customer testimonials in a horizontal slider format to build trust and credibility. It highlights real feedback from both residential and business clients.

The slider displays multiple testimonial cards at once and allows smooth horizontal scrolling. Users can navigate through testimonials using swipe gestures or arrow controls. The slider can also support auto-play for continuous movement.

Each testimonial card includes a short customer quote, their name, role or designation, star rating, and an optional profile image. The design uses clean cards with rounded corners, subtle shadows, and proper spacing to ensure a modern and user-friendly appearance.

This section helps improve user confidence by presenting authentic customer experiences and strengthens the overall reliability of the service.
